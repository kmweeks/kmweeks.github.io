Just enter the text you want to read and choose the speed. You can even set the number of words to display at a time.

Speed Reading is great way to get the most out of your time. With very little effort, you can double or even triple your reading speed!

This app helps train you to speed read by forcing you to focus entirely on the text and maintaining a specific speed.
