The purpose of the site is to connect the business with people who have bikes and bikes parts to sell, or those seeking to buy specific parts. The key is providing the right information to the right person, and providing forms to help speed up the process of buying and selling. Take a look!

Because visitors to the site may want opposite things (buy or sell) it is important that they be led to the proper forms or links.
